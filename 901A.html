Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tracker work all over Pakistan?

Yes. It works anywhere there is GSM mobile network coverage.


Which SIM cards are supported?

Jazz, Zong, Ufone and Telenor SIM cards are all supported.


Can I monitor my vehicle from my phone?

Yes. You can monitor live location using Android, iPhone, Website or SMS.


Can I remotely stop my vehicle?

Yes. When the optional relay is installed, the ST-901A supports remote engine cut-off.


Is there any monthly tracker fee?

No. There is no subscription for the tracker itself. Only your SIM card's internet/SMS charges apply.
